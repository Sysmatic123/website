IR SENSOR

An IR (infrared) sensor block diagram illustrates the key components that work together to detect and respond to infrared light. For a reflective-type IR proximity sensor, the block diagram shows how an IR transmitter (an LED) emits light, and an IR receiver (a photodiode) detects the reflected light.
